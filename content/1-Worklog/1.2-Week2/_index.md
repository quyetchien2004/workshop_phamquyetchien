### Week 2 Achievements:

* Understood the purpose of AWS Free Tier for new AWS learners: it allows practice with low cost or no cost within the allowed limits.
* Gained a basic understanding that AWS Free Tier 2025 includes initial credits, additional credits from learning tasks, Free Plan/Paid Plan options, and Always Free services.
* Differentiated at a basic level that Free Plan is more suitable for beginners who want a safer learning path, while Paid Plan requires more caution because charges may occur.
* Became aware of the importance of checking billing, usage, and free usage limits to avoid unexpected charges.
* Learned that before practicing with an AWS service, it is necessary to check the region, free usage limits, created resources, and how to delete resources after use.
* Built an initial cost-safe AWS learning checklist:
  * Read the documentation before taking action.
  * Practice only within the required learning scope.
  * Check billing and usage regularly.
  * Delete unused resources.
* Prepared the foundation needed to start learning about AWS accounts, the AWS Management Console, and basic operations in the next week.
